# JESUS CHRIST: THE JUDGE OF ALL – INCLUDING MUSLIMS, MUHAMMAD, JINNS, AND ALLAH

**Source:** [Max Shimba Ministries](https://www.maxshimbaministries.org/2025/07/jesus-christ-judge-of-all-including.html)

**Category:** 21 Max Shimba Ministries

---

<div class="post-body entry-content" id="post-body-8099899761051454827" itemprop="description articleBody">
<p></p>
<p><em>By Dr. Maxwell Shimba, Servant of Jesus Christ</em></p>
<p><strong>Date:</strong> Monday, September 26, 2016</p>
<h3>Abstract:</h3>
<p>This article presents a biblical argument affirming that Jesus Christ is the ultimate and final Judge of all humanity. According to Scripture, Jesus will judge not only Christians but also Muslims, their prophet Muhammad, the jinn (supernatural beings in Islamic theology), and even Allah – who, from a Christian theological perspective, is not the true God. The message is a clarion call to all people, especially Muslims, to acknowledge Jesus Christ as Lord and Judge and to submit to His authority before the day of judgment.</p>
<hr/>
<h3>Introduction:</h3>
<p>One of the most critical questions of eschatology is: <strong>Who will judge the world?</strong> The Bible provides a clear and unambiguous answer: <strong>Jesus Christ is the appointed Judge of all humanity</strong>. This includes every man, woman, and spiritual being — Muslims, Muhammad, all jinns, and any being presumed divine by human traditions, including Allah as portrayed in Islamic theology.</p>
<hr/>
<h3>Biblical Evidence:</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Psalm 58:11</strong><br/>
<em>“Then people will say, ‘Surely the righteous still are rewarded; surely there is a God who judges the earth.’”</em><br/>
This passage affirms that God actively judges the earth, pointing toward divine justice through a personal Judge.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Revelation 18:8</strong><br/>
<em>“Therefore in one day her plagues will overtake her: death, mourning and famine. She will be consumed by fire, for mighty is the Lord God who judges her.”</em><br/>
This verse confirms that judgment will come suddenly by the hand of the Lord who has been given authority to judge.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>John 5:22</strong><br/>
<em>“Moreover, the Father judges no one, but has entrusted all judgment to the Son.”</em><br/>
Jesus Himself affirms that God the Father has delegated all judgment to Him, the Son — removing all ambiguity about who the final Judge will be.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Matthew 25:31–32</strong><br/>
<em>“When the Son of Man comes in His glory, and all the angels with Him, He will sit on His glorious throne. All the nations will be gathered before Him, and He will separate the people one from another as a shepherd separates the sheep from the goats.”</em><br/>
This passage reveals that Jesus, referred to as the “Son of Man,” will preside as Judge over all nations and people.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Acts 10:42</strong><br/>
<em>“He commanded us to preach to the people and to testify that He is the one whom God appointed as judge of the living and the dead.”</em><br/>
The Apostle Peter testifies to Jesus’ divine appointment as Judge over both the living and the dead.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Romans 2:16</strong><br/>
<em>“This will take place on the day when God judges people’s secrets through Jesus Christ, as my gospel declares.”</em><br/>
The apostle Paul teaches that all human secrets will be judged through Jesus Christ, not any other deity or prophet.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>2 Timothy 4:1</strong><br/>
<em>“In the presence of God and of Christ Jesus, who will judge the living and the dead, and in view of His appearing and His kingdom, I give you this charge.”</em><br/>
Paul again affirms Jesus Christ’s role in judging all humanity.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<hr/>
<h3>Conclusion:</h3>
<p>According to the consistent witness of Scripture, <strong>Jesus Christ alone is the appointed Judge of all creation</strong> — not Muhammad, not any prophet, not the jinn, and certainly not Allah as defined in Islamic doctrine. The Judge is Jesus, the Son of God, to whom all power and authority in heaven and on earth have been given.</p>
<p>Therefore, the question to every reader, especially to Muslims, is: <strong>Why not accept Jesus now, before the Day of Judgment arrives?</strong><br/>
The one who will judge your soul is not Muhammad nor Allah — it is <strong>Jesus Christ</strong>.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>Come to Jesus Today</strong><br/>
He is not just a prophet — He is your Savior, Lord, and Judge.</p>
