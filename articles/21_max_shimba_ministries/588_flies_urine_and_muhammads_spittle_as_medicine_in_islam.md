<h4 class="" data-end="325" data-start="286"><strong data-end="325" data-start="291">Muhammad Says Flies Are a Cure</strong></h4>
<p class="" data-end="547" data-start="326">"If a fly falls into the drink of anyone of you, it is essential to dip it (in the drink), for one of its wings has a disease and the other has the cure (antidote for the disease)." (Bukhari Volume 4, Book 54, Hadith 531)</p>
<p class="" data-end="792" data-start="549">"Abu Huraira narrated: The Prophet said, 'If a housefly falls into the drink of any of you, he should dip it in the drink, for one of its wings has a disease and the other has the cure for the disease.'" (Bukhari Volume 4, Book 54, Hadith 537)</p>
<p class="" data-end="924" data-start="794">For information, a well-fed fly defecates every five minutes according to <a class="" data-end="923" data-start="868" href="http://www.thebestcontrol.com/bugstop/control_flies.htm" rel="noopener" target="_new">http://www.thebestcontrol.com/bugstop/control_flies.htm</a>.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1462" data-start="926"><strong data-end="963" data-start="926">Abu Huraira Has a Perfect Memory:</strong><br/>
"Abu Huraira narrated: I said, 'O Allah's Messenger! I hear many narrations (Hadiths) from you but I forget them.' Allah's Messenger said, 'Spread your garment.' I did accordingly and then he moved his hands as if scooping something and emptied it into my garment and said, 'Take and wrap this sheet over your body.' I did it and after that I never forgot anything." (Bukhari Volume 1, Book 3, Hadith 119). Also found in Bukhari Volume 4, Book 56, Hadith 841; Bukhari Volume 9, Book 23, Hadith 452.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1761" data-start="1464">"Abu Huraira narrated: The Prophet said, 'If a fly falls in the vessel of any of you, let him dip all of it (into the vessel) and then throw it away, for in one of its wings there is a disease and in the other there is healing (antidote for the disease).'" (Bukhari Volume 7, Book 71, Hadith 673).</p>
<p class="" data-end="2020" data-start="1763">The footnotes explain that modern medicine has found that flies carry pathogens on some parts of their bodies, but they also carry antidotes for these pathogens. This explanation suggests that dipping the fly releases the antidote to counteract the disease.</p>
<h4 class="" data-end="2077" data-start="2022"><strong data-end="2077" data-start="2027">Muhammad Says Drinking Camel Urine Is Medicine</strong></h4>
<p class="" data-end="2283" data-start="2078"><strong data-end="2110" data-start="2078">Using Camel Urine as a Cure:</strong><br/>
According to Muhammad, people are punished in their graves for defiling themselves with urine. (Bukhari Volume 1, Book 4, Hadith 215; Bukhari Volume 1, Book 4, Hadith 217).</p>
<p class="" data-end="2478" data-start="2285">However, Muhammad instructed some people to follow his shepherd and drink the camel's milk and urine as a remedy. (Bukhari Volume 7, Book 71, Hadith 589, 590 and Volume 7, Book 71, Hadith 623).</p>
<p class="" data-end="2579" data-start="2480">Muhammad also said that camel urine is a good remedy. (Ibn-i-Majah Volume 5, Book 31, Hadith 3503).</p>
<p class="" data-end="2844" data-start="2581">Some Muslims were told to drink camel urine and ended up rebelling and killing the shepherd. Muhammad ordered their hands and feet to be cut off and their eyes to be branded. (Sunan Nasa’i Volume 1, Hadith 308-309). See also Bukhari Volume 4, Book 52, Hadith 261.</p>
<p class="" data-end="3033" data-start="2846"><strong data-end="2905" data-start="2846">Religious Incantations as a Remedy for Scorpion Stings:</strong><br/>
Muhammad used incantations to remove the poison from scorpion stings. (Sahih Muslim Volume 3, Book 24, Hadith 5442-5444, 5448).</p>
