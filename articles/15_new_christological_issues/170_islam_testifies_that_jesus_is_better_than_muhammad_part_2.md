**Concluding Remarks**

Our examination has shown that even Islam itself recognizes that Jesus Christ is in a class all by himself. The Islamic corpus explicitly testifies that Christ is greater than all creation, and vastly superior to and better than Muhammad himself. The Islamic data bears witness that Muhammad (to adopt the words of John the Baptist) isn’t worthy to stoop down and untie the strap of Jesus’ sandals or even carry them (cf. Mark 1:7; Matthew 3:11; Luke 3:15-16). 

Hence, the Islamic sources themselves refute the lie that Muhammad is the perfect and greatest man that has ever lived. According to Islam itself, that honor belongs to Jesus Christ alone. 

Now just to make things clear, we do not believe that the Quran or Muhammad’s sunna are revelations from God; nor do we believe that the Muslim Isa is the same as the historical Jesus revealed in God’s inspired Scriptures, specifically the writings of the Old and New Testaments. The Quranic Isa is a satanic counterfeit created to deceive people away from the real Jesus and his message of salvation, which can only be found in the Holy Bible (cf. 2 Corinthians 11:2-4, 13-15; Galatians 1:8-9). The only reason we are contrasting the Quranic teaching concerning Jesus with Muhammad is to show that even this counterfeit version of Christ outshines the one whom Muslims have been deceived into thinking is a messenger of the true God. 

Our hope is that the risen Lord of glory, God’s beloved Son, will use this contrast to draw people to God’s inspired Word, the Holy Bible, so that they can learn about the real Jesus. Once Muslims turn to the Holy Bible they will be able to see for themselves that the Christ of history is the eternal Word and beloved Son of God who became man in order to procure eternal redemption for all who would turn to him and confess him as their risen Lord and Savior. They will discover that the Lord Jesus loves them so much that he came to offer his life as a ransom in order to save all who would truly believe in him from God’s wrath which will soon be poured out upon the wicked:
