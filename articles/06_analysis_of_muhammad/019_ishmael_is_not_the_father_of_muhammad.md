While he was living in the Desert of Paran, his mother got a wife for him from Egypt**." Genesis 21:13-21 

The Holy Bible locates Paran near Israel (Canaan) and Egypt, south of Judah. John L. McKenzie in his _Dictionary of the Bible_ notes:
