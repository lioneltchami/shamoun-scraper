# The Prophets and their bodies: 

**More proof that Muhammad was a false prophet**

[Sam Shamoun](https://www.youtube.com/@shamounian)

This will be short and to the point. According to the ahadith, Muhammad claimed that Allah would never allow his body to decay but will make sure it remains incorruptible, something that Allah has sworn to do for the bodies of all the prophets:

**It was narrated from Abu Darda’ that the Messenger of Allah said:**

“Send a great deal of blessing upon me on Fridays, for it is witnessed by the angels. No one sends blessing upon me but his blessing will be presented to me, until he finishes them.” A man said: “Even after death?” He said: “Even after death, for Allah has forbidden the earth to consume the bodies of the Prophets, so the Prophet of Allah is alive and receives provision.’” 

**Grade: Da’if** (Darussalam) 

English reference: Vol. 1, Book 6, Hadith 1637 

Arabic reference: Book 6, Hadith 1706 ([Sunnah.com](<https://sunnah.com/urn/1289870> "Opens external link in new window"))

And: 

**Narrated Aws ibn Aws:**

The Prophet said: Among the most excellent of your days is Friday; on it Adam was created, on it he died, on it the last trumpet will be blown, and on it the shout will be made, so invoke more blessings on me that day, for your blessings will be submitted to me. The people asked: Messenger of Allah, how can it be that our blessings will be submitted to you while your body is decayed? He replied: Allah, the Exalted, has prohibited the earth from consuming the bodies of Prophets. 

**Grade: SAHIH** (Al-Albani) 

Reference: Sunan Abi Dawud 1047 

In-book reference: Book 2, Hadith 658 

English translation: Book 3, Hadith 1042 ([Sunna.com](<https://sunnah.com/abudawud/2/658> "Opens external link in new window"); capital emphasis ours) 

Suffice it to say, this serves as further proof that Muhammad was a false prophet, since the Holy Bible attests that the bodies of the true prophets of God do in fact decay, such as we find in the case of Joseph,

“Joseph said to his brothers, ‘I am about to die. God will surely come to you and bring you out of this land to the land of which He swore to Abraham, to Isaac, and to Jacob.’ Then Joseph made the sons of Israel swear, saying, ‘God will surely visit you, and you shall CARRY UP MY BONES from here.’ So Joseph died at the age of one hundred and ten years old, and they embalmed him, and he was put in a coffin in Egypt.” 50:24-26 

“Moses TOOK THE BONES OF JOSEPH with him, for he had made the children of Israel solemnly swear, saying, ‘God will surely attend to you, and you shall CARRY MY BONES AWAY from here with you.’ They took their journey from Sukkoth and camped in Etham, on the edge of the wilderness.” Exodus 13:19-20 

And Elisha:

“So Elisha died, and they buried him. Now Moabite raiders would enter the land in the spring.**** As they were burying a man, they saw raiders. So they threw the man into the tomb of Elisha. When the man touched THE BONES OF ELISHA, he came to life and stood on his feet.” 2 Kings 13:20-21
