<p><strong>Friday, December 2, 2016</strong><br/><br/></p>
<p>Read the authentic hadith from Al-Bukhari:<br/>
Muhammad says this in (Bukhari Hadith number 425, volume 3), the hadith narrated by Abu Huraira, the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) said:<br/>
He said, "I swear by the One in whose hand my soul is, soon the Son of Mary will descend among you and will judge with justice."<br/>
If Muhammad swears by Jesus and acknowledges that Jesus will be the one to judge, why should I follow Allah, who is not Yahweh?<br/>
Why didn’t Muhammad swear by Allah?<br/>
NOW, IF ONE MUST SWEAR ONLY BY ALMIGHTY GOD, WHY DID MUHAMMAD SWEAR BY JESUS?<br/>
SO, JESUS IS ALMIGHTY GOD.</p>
<p>In the hadith narrated by Abdallah bin `Umar (may Allah be pleased with him) and reported by Al-Bukhari:<br/>
"إِنَّ اللَّهَ يَنْهَاكُمْ أَنْ تَحْلِفُوا بِآبَائِكُمْ ، فَمَنْ كَانَ حَالِفًا فَلْيَحْلِفْ بِاللَّهِ أو فَلْيَصْمُتْ"<br/>
Its meaning: “Indeed, Allah forbids you from swearing by your fathers. Whoever has to swear, let him swear by Allah or remain silent.”</p>
<p>NOW, READ WHERE THE PROPHET MUHAMMAD SWEARS BY JESUS:<br/>
(Bukhari Hadith number 425, volume 3), the hadith narrated by Abu Huraira, the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) said:<br/>
He said, "I swear by the One in whose hand my soul is, soon the Son of Mary will descend among you and will judge with justice."</p>
<p>SO, MUHAMMAD KNOWS THAT JESUS IS ALMIGHTY GOD.</p>
