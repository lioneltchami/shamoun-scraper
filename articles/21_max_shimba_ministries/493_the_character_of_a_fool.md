<strong>THE CHARACTER OF A FOOL</strong></p>
<p><strong>Psalm 53:1; Psalm 107:17; Psalm 74:22; Psalm 10:4; Proverbs 10:23; Romans 3:10</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><em>The fool says in his heart, “There is no God.”<br/>
They are corrupt, and their ways are evil;<br/>
there is none who does good.</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>NOW READ ABOUT THE FOOL HERE:</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Shahada:</strong> <em>“La ilaha illallah Muhammadur Rasulullah”</em> (Qur’an 3:18; also see <em>Sahih al-Bukhari</em>, Volume 4, Book 56, Number 725).<br/>
“There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is the messenger of Allah.”<br/>
This is the Shahada recited by Muslims or by anyone wishing to become a Muslim.</p>
<p>The four words <em>“la ilaha illallah”</em> mean as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>la</strong> = <em>no</em>, <em>not</em>, <em>none</em>, <em>neither</em></p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>ilaha</strong> = <em>god</em>, <em>deity</em>, <em>object of worship</em></p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>illa</strong> = <em>except</em>, <em>but</em> (a contraction of <em>in-lâ</em>, literally “if not”)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Allah</strong> = <em>Allah</em></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Therefore</strong>, beginning with Allah, Muhammad, and those who recite the Shahada — according to the Psalmist’s biblical definition — all are described as <strong>fools</strong>, for they deny the true God of the Bible.</p>
<p><strong>Shalom.</strong></p>
