Abu Talib himself breastfed him and Allah sent milk through his nipples. He continued to be breasted by him for several days until Abu Talib found Halima al-Sa‘diyyah, so he gave him to her.”</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">Sahih al-Kafi Shaykh Baqir al-Behbudi</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">Mirʾāt al-ʿUqūl fī Sharḥ Akhbār Āl al-Rasūl (5/252) Allamah Baqir al-Majlisi (<a href="https://thaqalayn.net/book/1" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Al-Kāfi – Volume 1</a>, <a href="https://thaqalayn.net/chapter/1/4/111" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Book 4, Chapter #111,</a> <a href="https://thaqalayn.net/chapter/1/4/111" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">The Birth of The Holy Prophet and His Demise</a>)</p></div>
