# He Who Comes –

**More Explicit Evidence for the Deity of Christ**

In this article we are going to present evidence to establish that both the Lord Jesus and his followers proclaimed the absolute Deity of Christ. We will demonstrate this by showing how the NT writings ascribe the very unique roles, titles, and functions of God Almighty to the Lord Jesus Christ, specifically in relation to God coming with his hosts to judge the nations. 

  
**Yahweh**

To begin with, Yahweh is said to be the First and the Last according to the Hebrew writings:

“This is what the Lord says—Israel’s King and Redeemer, the Lord Almighty: _I am the first and I am the last_ ; apart from me there is no God.” Isaiah 44:6

“Listen to me, Jacob, Israel, whom I have called: _I am he; I am the first and I am the last_.” Isaiah 48:12

The Hebrew Bible also testifies that Yahweh rides the clouds, e.g. that the clouds are his chariot so to speak:

“Moses and Aaron, Nadab and Abihu, and the seventy elders of Israel went up _and saw the God of Israel. Under HIS FEET was something like a pavement made of lapis lazuli, as bright blue as the sky_. But God did not raise his hand against these leaders of the Israelites; _they saw God_ , and they ate and drank… When Moses went up on the mountain, _the cloud covered it, and the glory of the Lord settled on Mount Sinai_. For six days the cloud covered the mountain, _and on the seventh day the Lord called to Moses FROM WITHIN THE CLOUD_. To the Israelites the glory of the Lord looked like a consuming fire on top of the mountain. Then Moses entered the cloud as he went on up the mountain. And he stayed on the mountain forty days and forty nights.” Exodus 24:9-11, 15-18

“There is no one like the God of Jeshurun, who rides across the heavens to help you _and on the clouds in his majesty_.” Deuteronomy 33:26 

“The Lord wraps himself in light as with a garment; he stretches out the heavens like a tent and lays the beams of his upper chambers on their waters. _He makes the clouds his chariot_ and rides on the wings of the wind.” Psalm 104:2-3

“The Lord is slow to anger but great in power; the Lord will not leave the guilty unpunished. His way is in the whirlwind and the storm, _and clouds are the dust of his feet_.” Nahum 1:3

We are further told that Yahweh will come with his heavenly hosts to rule and judge the nations:

“You who bring good news to Zion, go up on a high mountain. You who bring good news to Jerusalem, lift up your voice with a shout, lift it up, do not be afraid; say to the towns of Judah, ‘ _Here is your God!_ ’ See, the _Sovereign Lord comes with power, and he rules with a mighty arm. “The Lord has made proclamation to the ends of the earth: Say to Daughter Zion, ‘ _See, your Savior comes! See, his reward is with him, and his recompense accompanies him_.’” Isaiah 62:11 – cf. Psalm 62:12; Proverbs 24:12; Jeremiah 11:20; 17:10; 29:23

This next one says that Yahweh will even split the Mount of Olives in half when his feet land upon it!

“ _A day of the Lord is coming, Jerusalem_ , when your possessions will be plundered and divided up within your very walls. **The Lord Jesus Christ**

Remarkably, the NT ascribes all of these unique functions and characteristics to the Lord Jesus Christ! 

For instance, it is Christ who, as the Son of Man and risen Lord, rides the clouds of heaven and comes with his angelic host to judge the nations. Christ is also the First and the Last who searches hearts, and tests the minds, in order to repay everyone according to their deeds. 

Here, we are going to cite references from several NT books with little to no commentary, so that the readers can see for themselves that both the Lord Jesus and his followers basically identify Christ as Yahweh God Incarnate since they attribute to him the very roles and titles which both the Hebrew Bible and the Quran agree are uniquely divine, and therefore can only be ascribed to God Almighty.

**Conclusion**

The testimony of Jesus and that of his followers is crystal clear. By claiming that Jesus is the First and the Last, and the risen Lord who comes on the clouds of heaven with all his angels in order to repay a person for what s/he has done, Christ and the NT writers were basically proclaiming that Jesus is God Almighty Incarnate. There is simply no way around this fact. 

What makes this all the more remarkable is that the book of Acts says that Christ will return from the place where he ascended, which happens to be the Mount of Olives, the very Mount which the prophet Zechariah saw Yahweh’s feet touch and split it in half upon landing!

“After he said this, he was taken up before their very eyes, and a cloud hid him from their sight. They were looking intently up into the sky as he was going, when suddenly two men dressed in white stood beside them. ‘Men of Galilee,’ they said, ‘why do you stand here looking into the sky? This same Jesus, who has been taken from you into heaven, _will come back in the same way you have seen him go into heaven_.’ Then the apostles returned to Jerusalem _from the hill called the Mount of Olives_ , a Sabbath day’s walk from the city.” Acts 1:9-12

This means that Zechariah was enabled by the Holy Spirit to basically see the risen Lord Jesus descend from heaven in his physical glorified body! 

In light of this, could the Holy Bible be any clearer that Jesus is Yahweh God in the flesh (even though he is not the Father or the Holy Spirit)?
