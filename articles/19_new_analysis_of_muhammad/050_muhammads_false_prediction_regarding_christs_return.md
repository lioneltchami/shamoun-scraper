The language of these reports is crystal clear. Muhammad was addressing his immediate followers and told them that “Jesus would SOON descend among YOU,” and “lead YOU,” meaning those he was addressing. It is therefore clear that Muhammad was limiting this to the generation of his own followers. However, this event never happened since Muhammad’s followers died and Jesus did not descend among them to lead them. 

What makes this even more interesting is that none of these ahadith say that Jesus would descend upon “the Muslims living at the time of his return” or any other general formulation in the third person, but always, in every single narration it uses the word, “you”, i.e. the direct address which is clearly intended to be understood personally. 

Now if these Muslim polemicists were consistent then they would have to apply their logic to these traditions and conclude that Muhammad must be a false prophet (which he is) because he claimed that Jesus would _SOON_ descend during the lifetime of his companions and that they would be able to recognize him when he did! 

They must face the fact that it has been nearly 15 centuries since Muhammad allegedly uttered these words, and not only have all his companions died but Christ has still not returned to the earth!
