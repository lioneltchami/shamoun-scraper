<p class="" data-end="407" data-start="209">Does a being who swears by another qualify as God or human? Who is the creator here? Because Allah swears by the one who created, and creation is a characteristic of God. Thus, Allah did not create.</p>
<p class="" data-end="528" data-start="409">Who is this who created Male and Female, to whom the Allah of Islam swears by? Continue reading... from the Holy Bible…</p>
<p class="" data-end="689" data-start="530"><strong data-end="548" data-start="530">Jeremiah 27:5:</strong> "With my great power and outstretched arm I made the earth and its people and the animals that are on it, and I give it to anyone I please."</p>
<p class="" data-end="965" data-start="691">Now we read that Jehovah, who is the God of the Bible, is the one who created everything, including male and female, to whom the Allah of Islam swears by. Therefore, Jehovah created male and female, and the Allah of Islam swears by Him, so determine for yourself who is God.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1020" data-start="967">Can God swear by another? Continue to gain knowledge…</p>
<p class="" data-end="1152" data-start="1022"><strong data-end="1039" data-start="1022">Hebrews 6:13:</strong> "When God made his promise to Abraham, since there was no one greater for him to swear by, he swore by himself,"</p>
<p class="" data-end="1290" data-start="1154">Jehovah says there is no one greater than Him to swear by, but Allah in the Quran says he swears by the one who created male and female.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1417" data-start="1292">Therefore, today we understand that the creator is Jehovah, and Allah swears by Jehovah, the God of the Bible and Christians.</p>
