<p class="" data-end="122" data-start="110">Dear Reader,</p>
<p class="" data-end="431" data-start="124">Muslims often assert that the Quran is a comprehensive book that explains everything and is self-sufficient, claiming it is a revelation from Almighty God, while criticizing the Bible as not being from God. I will present two verses from the Bible and then two verses from the Quran, followed by a question.</p>
<p class="" data-end="485" data-start="433"><strong data-end="485" data-start="433">WHEN DID THE FLOOD OCCUR ACCORDING TO THE BIBLE?</strong></p>
<p class="" data-end="575" data-start="487"><strong data-end="502" data-start="487">Genesis 7:6</strong> "Noah was six hundred years old when the floodwaters were on the earth."</p>
<p class="" data-end="750" data-start="577">The Bible, which is claimed not to be from God, states that Noah was six hundred (600) years old when the flood occurred. It also explains the years of Noah after the flood.</p>
<p class="" data-end="832" data-start="752"><strong data-end="768" data-start="752">Genesis 9:28</strong> "And Noah lived after the flood three hundred and fifty years."</p>
<p class="" data-end="868" data-start="834"><strong data-end="868" data-start="834">HOW OLD WAS NOAH WHEN HE DIED?</strong></p>
<p class="" data-end="960" data-start="870"><strong data-end="886" data-start="870">Genesis 9:29</strong> "So all the days of Noah were nine hundred and fifty years; and he died."</p>
<p class="" data-end="1216" data-start="962">We are told by the Bible that Noah lived three hundred and fifty (350) years after the flood, and he died at the age of nine hundred and fifty (950) years. This is the total number of years Noah lived. Now let's turn to the Quran and then ask a question.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1270" data-start="1218"><strong data-end="1270" data-start="1218">WHEN DID THE FLOOD OCCUR ACCORDING TO THE QURAN?</strong></p>
<p class="" data-end="1544" data-start="1272"><strong data-end="1290" data-start="1272">Quran 29:14-15</strong><br/>
14. "And indeed We sent Noah to his people, and he stayed among them a thousand years less fifty years; so the flood overtook them while they were wrongdoers."<br/>
15. "But We saved him and the companions of the ship, and We made it a sign for the worlds."</p>
<p class="" data-end="1574" data-start="1546"><strong data-end="1574" data-start="1546">THE COMPLETE DISCUSSION:</strong></p>
<p class="" data-end="1948" data-start="1576">The Bible says that the flood occurred when Noah was 600 years old. The Quran says that the flood occurred when Noah was 950 years old, which corresponds to the age at which Noah died according to the Bible. The Bible says that Noah lived 350 years after the flood and died at the age of 950 years, which matches the age at which the flood occurred according to the Quran.</p>
