# WHY DOES ALLAH HATE CHRISTIANS AND JEWS?

---

<p><em>By Dr. Maxwell Shimba | Shimba Theological Institute | Max Shimba Ministries Org ©2016</em></p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>Introduction</strong></p>
<p>Dear reader,</p>
<p>Today, I would like us to reflect on a serious theological issue: the apparent hatred found within Islam, particularly in the Qur’an, directed at Christians and Jews. Many people mistakenly assume that the God of the Bible, full of love, is the same as Allah of the Qur’an. This, however, is a grave misconception.</p>
<p>The God revealed in the Bible is characterized by love and grace, while the Allah of the Qur’an, as we shall demonstrate, promotes hostility, hatred, and division. The Qur’an, which is the central text of Islam, shows that Allah commands his followers to harbor animosity toward Christians and Jews.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>1. Allah Forbids Friendship with Christians and Jews</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Qur’an, Surah Al-Ma’idah 5:51</strong><br/>
<em>"O you who have believed, do not take the Jews and the Christians as allies. They are [in fact] allies of one another. And whoever is an ally to them among you – then indeed, he is [one] of them. Indeed, Allah guides not the wrongdoing people."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>If Allah is the same as Yahweh, the loving God of the Bible, why then does he forbid friendship with people of other faiths?</p>
<p>Compare this to the commandment of Jesus Christ:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Luke 6:35 (Holy Bible – SNT)</strong><br/>
<em>"But love your enemies, do good to them, and lend to them without expecting to get anything back. Then your reward will be great, and you will be children of the Most High, because He is kind to the ungrateful and wicked."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>Clearly, the teachings of Jesus and the commands of Allah are diametrically opposed. The God of the Bible teaches us to love even our enemies. Allah promotes hatred.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>2. Allah Forbids Muslims from Loving Christians – Even Close Family</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah Al-Mujādilah 58:22</strong><br/>
<em>"You will not find a people who believe in Allah and the Last Day having affection for those who oppose Allah and His Messenger, even if they were their fathers or their sons or their brothers or their kindred..."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>This verse teaches that Muslims must not love those who do not follow Islam, even if they are family members. Such a position is starkly different from the teachings of the Bible, which emphasizes compassion, mercy, and familial responsibility.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>3. Allah Orders Muslims Not to Associate with Jews and Christians</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah An-Nisa 4:144</strong><br/>
<em>"O you who have believed, do not take the disbelievers as allies instead of the believers. Do you wish to offer Allah a clear case against yourselves?"</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>This kind of command encourages religious segregation and conflict. By contrast, the Bible repeatedly encourages unity, peace, and love for all people.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>4. Allah Warns Against Trusting Non-Muslims</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah Al-Imran 3:118</strong><br/>
<em>"O you who have believed, do not take those outside your religion as confidants. They will not fail to ruin you; they wish you would have hardship. Hatred has already appeared from their mouths, and what their hearts conceal is greater..."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>Again, this verse reflects a deep mistrust and animosity toward non-Muslims, which contradicts the character of God in the Bible who instructs us to walk in love and truth.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>5. Allah Declares Disbelievers as Open Enemies</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah An-Nisa 4:101</strong><br/>
<em>"And when you travel throughout the land, there is no blame upon you for shortening the prayer [especially] if you fear that those who disbelieve may harm you. Indeed, the disbelievers are ever to you a clear enemy."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>This verse classifies all non-Muslims as enemies, further fueling religious division. Yet the God of the Bible declares:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>John 10:10-11</strong><br/>
<em>"The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I came that they may have life and have it abundantly. I am the good shepherd. The good shepherd lays down His life for the sheep."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>The Lord Jesus brings life, not death. He heals, unites, and restores. The contrast is undeniable.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>6. Allah Commands Muslims to Fight and Kill Disbelievers</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah At-Tawbah 9:123</strong><br/>
<em>"O you who have believed, fight those adjacent to you of the disbelievers and let them find in you harshness. And know that Allah is with the righteous."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>Violence is normalized and even glorified under this command. Conversely, Jesus taught that <strong>"Blessed are the peacemakers, for they shall be called sons of God"</strong> (Matthew 5:9).</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>7. Allah Declares Christians and Jews as Enemies Until They Convert</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>Surah Al-Mumtahanah 60:4</strong><br/>
<em>"...We disbelieve in you, and there has appeared between us and you animosity and hatred forever until you believe in Allah alone..."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>This eternal hatred is entirely contrary to the heart of God revealed in Christ, who loves unconditionally and calls all people to repentance through grace.</p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>Jesus the Giver of Abundant Life</strong></p>
<p>Jesus, the Son of God, said:</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>John 10:10</strong><br/>
<em>"The thief comes only to steal and kill and destroy; I came that they may have life and have it abundantly."</em></p>
</blockquote>
<p>Satan's agenda has always been destruction. But Jesus came to bring life in its fullness—restoring, healing, and blessing every area of life: family, business, health, and relationships.</p>
<p>If the enemy has stolen or destroyed anything in your life, remember: <strong>Jesus came so you may have life—life in abundance.</strong></p>
<hr/>
<p><strong>Final Exhortation</strong></p>
<p>Dear beloved,</p>
<p>Do not allow hatred to rule your heart. Choose the path of love, peace, and salvation through Jesus Christ. The character of Allah, as seen in the Qur’an, bears no resemblance to the loving God revealed in the Holy Bible. Allah’s attributes reflect those of Satan—the deceiver, the divider, the destroyer. But Jesus Christ is the Good Shepherd, the Savior, and the Author of Life.</p>
<p>If you feel convicted and want to surrender your life to Jesus, reach out to us. We will help you take the most important step in your life.</p>
<p><strong>Jesus loves you.</strong></p>
