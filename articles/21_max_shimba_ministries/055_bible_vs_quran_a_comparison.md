<p></p><h2>📚 Bible vs 📙 Quran – A Comparison</h2><h3>1. <strong>Authorship</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: Written by <strong>40+ known authors</strong> (prophets, kings, fishermen, doctors, etc.) over <strong>1,400 years</strong>, across <strong>3 continents</strong> (Asia, Africa, Europe), in <strong>3 languages</strong> (Hebrew, Aramaic, Greek).</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: Attributed to <strong>one man</strong> (Muhammad), compiled by his followers after his death, with <strong>uncertain textual history</strong> and <strong>no independent witnesses</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr/><h3>2. <strong>Size (Word Count)</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: <strong>773,746 words</strong> (English KJV count).</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: <strong>77,430 words</strong> (Arabic).<br/>➡️ The Bible is about <strong>10 times larger</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr/><h3>3. <strong>Chapters (Structure)</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: <strong>1,189 chapters</strong> (Old Testament – 929; New Testament – 260).</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: <strong>114 surahs (chapters)</strong>.</p></li></ul><hr/><h3>4. <strong>Verses</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: <strong>31,102 verses</strong>.</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: About <strong>6,236 verses</strong> (including Bismillah counts, sometimes disputed).</p></li></ul><hr/><h3>5. <strong>Content</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: Covers <strong>creation, history, prophecy, law, poetry, wisdom, gospels, letters, and revelation</strong>. Contains <strong>3,000+ fulfilled prophecies</strong> and detailed history spanning thousands of years.</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: Mostly <strong>repetitive commands, borrowed stories, and legal rulings</strong>. Very few prophecies, no coherent timeline, and many contradictions.</p></li></ul><hr/><h3>6. <strong>Message</strong></h3><ul><li><p><strong>Bible</strong>: Unified story of God’s love, pointing to salvation through <strong>Jesus Christ</strong> from Genesis to Revelation.</p></li><li><p><strong>Quran</strong>: Emphasizes <strong>submission to Allah</strong>, without a consistent theme of redemption or grace.</p></li></ul><hr/><p>✅ <strong>Conclusion</strong>:<br/>The <strong>Bible</strong> is a vast, multi-authored, historically grounded, prophetic, and spiritually deep collection of writings inspired by God. The <strong>Quran</strong> is a much smaller, one-man narrative, limited in scope, and incomparable to the richness and authority of the Bible.</p><hr/><p></p><h1>📚 The Bible vs 📙 The Quran – A Debate</h1><h3>Opening Statement</h3><p>You cannot compare the Bible and the Quran.
