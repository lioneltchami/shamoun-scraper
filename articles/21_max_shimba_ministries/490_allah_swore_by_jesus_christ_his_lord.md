<p>First, let's learn the meaning of swearing so that we can understand why Allah swore by Jehovah.</p><p class="" data-end="164" data-start="40">
What does it mean to swear?</p>
<p class="" data-end="533" data-start="166">Swearing, or taking an oath, means affirming, using the Name of Almighty God, that something being sworn to - and which has no clear evidence - is true and correct as claimed by the one taking the oath, and thus it must be believed that those claims are true because the Ultimate Guarantor is Almighty God, the Only Knower of the truth or falsehood of hidden matters!</p>
<p class="" data-end="731" data-start="535">In a Hadith narrated by Abdullah bin `Umar R.A.A. and reported by Al-Bukhari,<br/>
"Indeed, Allah prohibits you from swearing by your fathers. So whoever must swear should swear by Allah or be silent."</p>
<p class="" data-end="811" data-start="733">Now let's read about Allah, who is not Jehovah "Yahweh", who does He swear by?</p>
<p class="" data-end="1131" data-start="813"><em data-end="855" data-start="813">Quran 91:1-7 Suratul Ash-Shams (The Sun)</em><br/>
By the sun and its brightness. And [by] the moon when it follows it. And [by] the day when it displays it. And [by] the night when it covers it. And [by] the sky and He who constructed it. And [by] the earth and He who spread it. And [by] the soul and He who proportioned it.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1283" data-start="1133"><em data-end="1175" data-start="1133">Quran 92:1-3 Suratul Al-Layl (The Night)</em><br/>
By the night when it covers. And [by] the day when it appears. And [by] He who created the male and female.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1415" data-start="1285">Here we see that Allah, worshipped by Muslims, swears by the sky and the one who built it, by the earth and the one who spread it.</p>
<p class="" data-end="1477" data-start="1417">Who is the Spreader of the earth and the Builder of the sky?</p>
<p class="" data-end="1743" data-start="1479">For Allah of Islam, swears by Him. Your answer is, God Jehovah created male and female, God Jehovah created Heaven and Earth, but Allah swears by the Creator of Heaven and Earth and by the Creator of male and female. Allah swears by Jehovah, the God of Christians.</p>
