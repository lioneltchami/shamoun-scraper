All emphasis will be mine.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;">The founder…</em></strong></p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">On his commercial journeys to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14399a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Syria</a> and Palestine <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">he became acquainted with <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08399a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Jews</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christians</a>, and acquired an imperfect <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08673a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">knowledge</a> of their religion and traditions</u></em></strong>. He was a man of retiring disposition, addicted to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12345b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">prayer</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05789c.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">fasting</a>, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">and was subject to epileptic fits</u></em></strong>. In his fortieth year (A.D. 612), he claimed to have received a call from the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06330a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Angel Gabriel</a>, and thus began his active career as the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12477a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">prophet</a> of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01316a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Allah</a> and the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01626c.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">apostle</a> of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arabia</a>… He took the field against his enemies, conquered several <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arabian</a>, <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08399a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Jewish</a>, and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christian</a> tribes, entered <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10099a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Mecca</a> in triumph in 630, demolished the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07636a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">idols</a> of the Kaaba, became master of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arabia</a>, and finally united all the tribes under one emblem and one religion. In 632 he made his last <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12085a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">pilgrimage</a> to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10099a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Mecca</a> at the head of forty thousand followers, and soon after his return died of a <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15446a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">violent</a> fever in the sixty-third year of his age, the eleventh of the Hejira, and the year 633 of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christian</a> era.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">The sources of Mohammed’s biography are numerous, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">but on the whole untrustworthy, being crowded with fictitious details, legends, and stories. None of his biographies were compiled during his lifetime, and the earliest was written a century and a half after his death</u></em></strong>. The <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08692a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Koran</a> is perhaps the only reliable source for the leading events in his career. His earliest and chief biographers are Ibn Ishaq (A.H. 151=A.D. 768), Wakidi (207=822), Ibn Hisham (213=828), Ibn Sa’d (230=845), Tirmidhi (279=892), Tabari (310-929), the “Lives of the Companions of Mohammed”, the numerous <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08692a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Koranic</a> commentators [especially Tabari, quoted above, Zamakhshari 538=1144), and Baidawi (691=1292)], the “Musnad”, or collection of traditions of Ahmad ibn Hanbal (241=855), the collections of Bokhari (256=870), the “Isabah”, or “Dictionary of Persons who knew Mohammed”, by Ibn Hajar, etc. All these collections and biographies are based on the so-called Hadiths, or “traditions”, the historical value of which is more than <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05141a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">doubtful</a>.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">These traditions, in fact, represent a gradual, and more or less artificial, legendary development, rather than supplementary historical information</u></em></strong>. According to them, Mohammed was simple in his habits, but most careful of his personal appearance. He loved perfumes and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07149b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">hated</a> strong drink. Of a highly nervous temperament, he shrank from bodily pain. Though gifted with great powers of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07672a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">imagination</a>, he was taciturn. He was affectionate and magnanimous, <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12748a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">pious</a> and austere in the practice of his religion, <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06147a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">brave</a>, <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15753a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">zealous</a>, and above reproach in his personal and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05782a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">family</a> conduct. <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">Palgrave, however, wisely remarks that “the ideals of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arab</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15472a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">virtue</a> were first conceived and then attributed to him</u></em></strong>“. Nevertheless, with every allowance for exaggeration, Mohammed is shown by his life and deeds to have been a man of dauntless <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06147a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">courage</a>, great generalship, strong <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03794b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">patriotism</a>, merciful by nature, and quick to forgive. <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">And yet he was ruthless in his dealings with the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08399a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Jews</a>, when once he had ceased to hope for their submission. He approved of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15108a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">assassination</a>, when it furthered his cause; however barbarous or treacherous the means, the end justified it in his eyes; and in more than one case he not only approved, but also instigated the crime</u></em></strong>.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">Concerning his <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10559a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">moral</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03584b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">character</a> and sincerity, contradictory opinions have been expressed by scholars in the last three centuries. Many of these opinions are biased either by an extreme <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07149b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">hatred</a> of Islam and its founder or by an exaggerated admiration, coupled with a <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07149b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">hatred</a> of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christianity</a>.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;"><a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09438b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Luther</a> looked upon him as “a <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/04764a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">devil</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06081a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">first-born</a> child of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/04764a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Satan</a>“. Maracci held that Mohammed and Mohammedanism were not very dissimilar to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09438b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Luther</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12495a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Protestantism</a></u></em></strong>. Spanheim and D’Herbelot characterize him <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">as a “wicked <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07698b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">impostor</a>“, and a “dastardly <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09469a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">liar</a></u></em></strong>“, while Prideaux stamps him <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">as a wilful deceiver</u></em></strong>. Such indiscriminate abuse is unsupported by facts.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">Modern scholars, such as Sprenger, Noldeke, Weil, Muir, Koelle, Grimme, Margoliouth, give us a more correct and unbiased estimate of Mohammed’s life and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03584b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">character</a>, and substantially agree as to his motives, prophetic call, personal qualifications, and sincerity. The various estimates of several recent critics have been ably collected and summarized by Zwemer, in his “Islam, a Challenge to Faith” (New York, 1907). According to Sir William Muir, Marcus Dods, and some others, Mohammed was at first sincere, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">but later, carried away by success, he practised <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09469a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">deception</a> wherever it would gain his end</u></em></strong>. Koelle “finds the key to the first period of Mohammed’s life in Khadija, his first wife”, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">after whose death he became a prey to his <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05649a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">evil</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/11534a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">passions</a>. Sprenger attributes the alleged <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13005a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">revelations</a> to epileptic fits, or to “a paroxysm of cataleptic <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08041a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">insanity</a></u></em></strong>“.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">Zwemer himself goes on to criticize the life of Mohammed by the standards, first, of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14526a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Old</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14530a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">New Testaments</a>, both of which Mohammed acknowledged as <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13001a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Divine revelation</a>; second, by the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/11388a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">pagan</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10559a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">morality</a> of his <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arabian</a> compatriots; lastly, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">by the new <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09053a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">law</a> of which he PRETENDED to be the “divinely appointed medium and custodian”. According to this author, the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12477a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">prophet</a> was <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05781a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">false</a> even to the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05556a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">ethical</a> traditions of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07636a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">idolatrous</a> brigands among whom he lived, and grossly violated the easy sexual <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10559a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">morality</a> of his own system</u></em></strong>. After this, it is hardly <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10733a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">necessary</a> to say that, in Zwemer’s opinion, <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">Mohammed fell very far short of the most elementary requirements of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13635b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Scriptural</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10559a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">morality</a></u></em></strong>. Quoting Johnstone, Zwemer concludes by remarking <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">that the judgment of these modern scholars, however harsh, rests on evidence which “comes all from the lips and the pens of his own devoted adherents. . .And the followers of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12477a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">prophet</a> can scarcely complain if, even on such evidence, the verdict of history goes against him</u></em></strong>“.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;">Tenets</em></strong></p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">The principal tents of Mohammedanism are laid down in the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08692a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Koran</a>. All <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/09580c.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">men</a> will have to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12792a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">rise from the dead</a> and submit to the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08552a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">universal judgment</a>. The <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12792a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Resurrection</a> will be general and will extend to all creatures — <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01476d.htm" style="background-color: transparent; They will be dragged by the scalp, flung into the fire, wrapped in garments of flame, and beaten with iron maces. When their skins are well burned, other skins will be given them for their greater torture</u></em></strong>. While the damnation of all <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08002b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">eternal</a>, the Moslems, who, though holding the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15073a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">true</a> religion, have been guilty of heinous <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14004b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">sins</a>, will be delivered from <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07207a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">hell</a> after expiating their crimes.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">The joys and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06585a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">glories</a> of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07170a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Paradise</a> are as fantastic and sensual as the lascivious <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arabian</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10321a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">mind</a> could possibly imagine</u></em></strong>. “As plenty of water is one of the greatest additions to the delights of the Bedouin <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/01663a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Arab</a>, the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08692a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Koran</a> often speaks of the rivers of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07170a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Paradise</a> as a principal ornament thereof; some of these streams flow with water, some with wine and others with honey, besides many other lesser springs and fountains, whose pebbles are rubies and emeralds, while their earth consists of camphor, their beds of musk, and their sides of saffron. <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Paradise</a>, the enjoyment of whose company will be the principal <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07131b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">felicity</a> of the faithful</u></em></strong>. These maidens are <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/04470a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">created</a> not of clay, as in the case of mortal <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/15687b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">women</a>, but of pure musk, and free from all natural impurities, defects, and inconveniences. They will be beautiful and modest and secluded from public view in pavilions of hollow pearls. <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">The pleasures of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14519a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Paradise</a> will be so overwhelming that <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06608a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">God</a> will give to everyone the potentialities of a hundred <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07762a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">individuals</a></u></em></strong>. To each <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07762a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">individuals</a> a large mansion will be assigned, and the very meanest will have at his disposal <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">at least 80,000 servants and seventy-two wives of the girls of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/07170a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Paradise</a></u></em></strong>. While eating they will be waited on by 300 attendants, the food being served in dishes of gold, whereof 300 shall be set before him at once, containing each a different kind of food, and an inexhaustible supply of wine and liquors. The magnificence of the garments and gems is conformable to the delicacy of their diet. For they will be clothed in the richest silks and brocades, and adorned with bracelets of gold and silver, and crowns set with pearls, and will make use of silken carpets, couches, pillows, etc., and in order that they may enjoy all these pleasures, <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06608a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">God</a> will grant them perpetual youth, beauty, and vigour. Music and singing will also be ravishing and everlasting” (Wollaston, “Muhammed, His Life and Doctrines”).</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;">The Mohammedan doctrine of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12378a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">predestination</a> is equivalent to <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05791a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">fatalism</a>. They <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/02408b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">believe</a> in <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06608a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">God’s</a> absolute decree and predetermination both of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06636b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">good</a> and of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05649a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">evil</a>; viz., whatever has been or shall be in the world, whether <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06636b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">good</a> or <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05649a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">bad</a>, proceeds entirely from the Divine will, and is irrevocably fixed and recorded from all <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05551b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">eternity</a>. <strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">The possession and the exercise of our own <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06259a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; The absurdity of this doctrine was felt by later Mohammedan <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14580a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">theologians</a>, who sought in vain by various subtile distinctions to minimize it.</p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;">Morals</em></strong></p><p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin: 1em 0px 1.1em;"><strong style="box-sizing: inherit; font-weight: bold;"><em style="box-sizing: inherit;"><u style="box-sizing: inherit;">It is hardly <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/10733a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">necessary</a> here to emphasize the fact that the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05556a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">ethics</a> of Islam are far inferior to those of <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/08537a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Judaism</a> and even more inferior to those of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14530a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">New Testament</a></u></em></strong>. Furthermore, we cannot agree with Noldeke when he maintains that, although in many respects the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05556a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">ethics</a> of Islam are not to be compared even with such <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christianity</a> as prevailed, and still prevails, in the East, nevertheless, in other points, the new <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05752c.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">faith</a> — simple, robust, in the vigour of its youth — far surpassed the religion of the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/14399a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Syrian</a> and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05329b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Egyptian</a> <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christians</a>, which was in a stagnating condition, and steadily sinking lower and lower into the depths of barbarism (op. cit., Wollaston, 71, 72). The history and the development, as well as the past and present religious, social, and <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05556a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">ethical</a> condition of all the <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/03712a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">Christian</a> nations and countries, no matter of what <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/13674a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">sect</a> or school they may be, as compared with these of the various Mohammedan countries, in all ages, is a sufficient refutation of Noldeke’s assertion. What is really <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/06636b.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">good</a> in Mohammedan <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/05556a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">ethics</a> is either commonplace or borrowed from some other <a href="https://www.newadvent.org/cathen/12738a.htm" style="background-color: transparent; border-bottom: 1px dotted rgb(170, 170, 170); box-sizing: inherit; color: #444444; text-decoration-line: none;">religions</a>, whereas what is characteristic IS NEARLY ALWAYS IMPERFECT OR WICKED</u></em></strong>.</p></div>
