<p></p><h1>Sermon: The False Trinity of the Qur’an</h1><p><em>By Dr. Maxwell Shimba</em><br/>
<em>Shimba Theological Institute</em></p><hr/><h3>Introduction</h3><p>Beloved in Christ, today we expose a great deception that has confused many. The Qur’an, in Surah al-Ma’idah 5:116, accuses Christians of worshiping a trinity of <strong>Allah, Jesus, and Mary</strong>. It claims that our Lord Jesus told people to take His mother as a deity. Let me say it clearly: <strong>this is a lie from the pit of hell!</strong></p><p>No Christian in history, no Church Father, no council, no denomination—Orthodox, Catholic, or Protestant—has ever declared Mary as God. She is blessed among women, yes; she is the mother of our Lord in His humanity, yes; but she is a servant of God, not part of the Godhead.</p><hr/><h3>The True Trinity</h3><p>The Bible is clear: <strong>One God in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.</strong></p><ul>
<li>
<p>Jesus commanded baptism <em>“in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit”</em> (Matthew 28:19).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Paul blessed the Church with <em>“the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, the love of God, and the fellowship of the Holy Spirit”</em> (2 Corinthians 13:14).</p>
</li>
</ul><p>Mary’s name is never in this divine formula. The Trinity is eternal, holy, and perfect — and Mary is a created being redeemed by her own Son.</p><hr/><h3>The Qur’an’s Error</h3><p>Now, if the Qur’an truly came from God, it could not misrepresent the faith of Christians. God is all-knowing. God is truth. God cannot lie.</p><p>But the Qur’an does lie. It puts words in the mouth of Jesus that He never spoke. It fabricates a false trinity. And by doing so, it exposes itself as <strong>a counterfeit revelation</strong>.</p><p>This means that Allah, the god of the Qur’an, is not the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. The god of the Qur’an is a deceiver. And Muhammad, who spread this deception, is not a prophet of God but a <strong>false prophet</strong>—for the Bible says in Deuteronomy 18:20: <em>“But the prophet who presumes to speak a word in my name that I have not commanded him to speak…that prophet shall die.”</em></p><hr/><h3>The Call to Truth</h3><p>Church, the truth remains unshaken:</p><ul>
<li>
<p><strong>God the Father</strong> — the Creator of heaven and earth.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>God the Son</strong> — Jesus Christ, eternal Word made flesh, our Savior and Redeemer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>God the Holy Spirit</strong> — the Comforter, our Guide, our Seal of salvation.</p>
</li>
</ul><p>This is the holy Trinity, the true God, revealed in Scripture, worshiped by the Church, and confessed by the saints throughout the ages.</p><p>Therefore, let us boldly proclaim: The Qur’an is false. Muhammad is not a prophet. And Allah is not the true God. The living God is revealed in Jesus Christ our Lord — the same yesterday, today, and forever.</p><hr/><h3>Conclusion &amp; Proclamation</h3><p>Brothers and sisters, lift your voices in faith:</p><ul>
<li>
<p>We do not worship Mary.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We do not worship a false trinity.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>We worship the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit — one God in three persons, blessed forever.</p>
</li>
</ul><p>Let the world hear it: <em>“Hear, O Israel: The Lord our God, the Lord is One”</em> (Deuteronomy 6:4), and that one Lord has revealed Himself fully in Jesus Christ.</p><p>Amen and Amen!</p><hr/><p>
