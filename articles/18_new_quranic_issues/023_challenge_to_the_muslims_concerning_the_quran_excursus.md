Related to the preceding is the sixth problem with this concept. Abrogation is nothing more than an expedient attempt of explaining away the major contradictions within the Quran. > “… The principle laid down in this passage - relating to the supersession of the Biblical dispensation by that of the Qur’an - **has given rise to an erroneous interpretation by many Muslim theologians**.
