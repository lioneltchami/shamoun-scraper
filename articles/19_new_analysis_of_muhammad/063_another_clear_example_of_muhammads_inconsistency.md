 _(a privilege for you only, not for the (rest of) the believers.)_ `Ikrimah said: “**This means, it is not permissible for anyone else to marry a woman who offers herself to him; if a woman offers herself to a man, it is not permissible for him (to marry her) unless he gives her something**.” This was also the view of Mujahid, Ash-Sha`bi and others.
